About

Graduate in arts, anthropology & design, Cedric-A found himself creating structures and digital ideas. Throwing and collapsing writing and visuals, he tries to find beauty in chaos and random shapes. Child of the digital age, Cedric-A works mainly with computer and graphics tablet. The process could also lead to short videos, sometimes with sound and music as well. Inspired by various artistic styles such as Brutalism, Minimalism, Surrealism or Abstract, he brings together all kind of shapes, colors, lines, and real images. He feeds his drawings with some random pieces and transform them into a final work.
